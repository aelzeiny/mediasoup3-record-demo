To fix the WebSocket connection issue, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Click the button below to open the WebSocket server URL.
Step 2: When the browser warns about the certificate, click "Advanced" and then "Proceed to localhost (unsafe)".
Step 3: Once you see a blank page or security warning, you've successfully accepted the certificate.
Step 4: Close that tab and return to your application. The WebSocket connection should now work.
